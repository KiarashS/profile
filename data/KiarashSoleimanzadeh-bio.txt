Kiarash Soleimanzadeh is a teaching assistant, researcher, and software engineer 
who aims to evolve, innovate, and inspire through technology. He received his BS 
degree in software engineering from Malayer University, Malayer, Hamedan, Iran, 
in 2014, and his MS degree in the Department of Computer and Information Technology 
Engineering from the Razi University, Kermanshah, Iran, in 2018. He is interested 
in applications of artificial intelligence in applications of computer engineering 
and computer science in healthcare (smart-healthcare) and natural language processing 
to improve quality of life.
